Minimal: low tissue trauma, no significant fluid or blood loss such as cataract & endoscopy. Mild: limited tissue injury with minimal systemic effects such as Inguinal hernia. Moderate: noticeable stress response, and potential hemodynamic changes such as Laparoscopic cholecystectomy. Severe: Highly invasive surgery with significant fluid shifts or blood loss such as extensive laparatomy.
